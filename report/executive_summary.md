## 2. Key Findings

### High Priority Findings
1. **Default Rate Overview**
   From 7561 customers, 2019 customers (26.7%) are identified as high risk with unpaid tagging.

2. **Regional Risk Distribution**
   Surabaya has the highest default rate at 27.4%, indicating geographic location is a risk factor.

### Additional Findings
1. **Age and Credit Risk Correlation**
   Customers with unpaid tagging have average age of 45.9 years, while paying customers average 45.6 years. Difference of 0.3 years indicates age is a significant factor.

2. **Income Impact on Credit Risk**
   Unpaid customers have average monthly income of Rp25,285,785, compared to Rp25,035,547 for paying customers. Lower income group shows -1.0% higher default risk.

3. **Balance and Default Risk**
   Unpaid customers maintain average balance of Rp63,234,778, while paying customers average Rp49,732,997. Lower balance correlates with -27.1% higher default risk.
